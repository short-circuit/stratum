Everything you need

A tool that grows with your knowledge

Stratum combines the best of modern PKM tools into one fast, offline-first package. No subscriptions. No lock-in.

🧱

Block Outliner

Every paragraph is an addressable block with a UUID. Indent, outdent, drag to reorder — your notes have structure.

🔗

Wiki-Links & Backlinks

Connect ideas with [[wiki-links]]. See linked and unlinked references in the backlinks panel.

🕸️

Knowledge Graph

An interactive force-directed graph of your entire vault. Find orphans, discover clusters, explore connections.

🔍

Full-Text Search

Tantivy-powered search returns results in under 100ms — even with 10,000+ notes. Tag search with #tag.

📊

Datalog Queries

Query your knowledge graph like a database. :find ?block :where [?b :block/marker "TODO"]

🤖

AI Assistant

Rewrite, summarize, structure, or interlink your notes. Bring your own LLM — Ollama, OpenAI, Anthropic, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

🔄

Git Sync

Auto-commit, push, pull. Your version history is built in. Your data is never locked into a proprietary database.

🧮

Math & Diagrams

KaTeX equations and Mermaid diagrams render inline. From quadratic formulas to flowcharts — right in your notes.

🎨

Whiteboards

Excalidraw spatial canvas for visual thinking. Architecture diagrams, mind maps, brainstorming — all saved as JSON in your vault.

🃏

Flashcards

Spaced repetition built in. Write question:: and answer:: in any block — Stratum generates review cards automatically.

📋

Templates

Reusable templates with variable substitution. Create a meeting notes template and apply it in one click.

🌐

Web Research

Multi-depth research via SearXNG. Stratum searches, reads, and synthesizes findings directly into your notes with citations.

🧱

Outliner that thinks like you do

Blocks, not paragraphs. Every unit of content has a UUID, can be nested, tagged, marked as a task, and referenced from anywhere. No more wrestling with document structure — just write and organize.

  • Indent, outdent, and drag to reorder blocks
  • Task markers: TODO, DOING, DONE with priorities
  • Block references ((uuid)) and page embeds
  • KaTeX math and Mermaid diagrams inline
🕸️

See your knowledge take shape

The graph view maps every [[wiki-link]] as a node in an interactive network. Nodes scale by connectivity, color by tag. Find orphaned notes, explore connected components, and navigate your knowledge graph by clicking.

  • Force-directed layout — drag nodes, zoom, pan
  • Connected components and orphan detection
  • Filter by search, toggle tag nodes
  • Click any node to navigate to that page
Built different

Why use Stratum?

Most PKM tools are built by companies that profit from your data. Stratum is built differently — by developers, for developers, with principles that put you first.

📄

Plain Markdown Files

Every note is a .md file on your filesystem. Open them in any text editor. If Stratum disappeared tomorrow, your notes survive intact.

🔒

Fully Offline

No cloud dependency. No always-online requirement. Every feature, including AI, works without an internet connection when using local models.

⚡

Fast at Scale

Built with Rust and Tantivy. Full-text search in under 100ms at 10k notes. The editor doesn't stutter. The graph doesn't lag. Memory stays under 80MB idle.

🔓

Zero Vendor Lock-In

No proprietary database format. No cloud sync dependency. Your data is a directory of Markdown files — the most portable format in existence.

Ready to take control of your knowledge?

No sign-up. No data collection. Download, build, or run from source. Your notes stay yours.
